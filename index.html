Built for Everyone

Cancer impacts millions across the world, and too often devastation follows in its wake. Cancer Spot is designed to benefit everyone who has been affected by this debilitating disease. Cancer Spot users sign up as either a fighter, survivor, or supporter, and discover a safe place to connect with, learn from, and support others experiencing the same struggles.

fighter

Cancer sucks! From the initial kick in the stomach that accompanies diagnosis, fighters find themselves in the trenches of the cancer battle.

The fight against cancer is one you can never prepare for, and it often leaves you feeling painfully isolated.

Cancer Spot allows those currently fighting the disease to get help from those who best understand their struggle: fellow cancer fighters and survivors. We connect you with fighters or survivors of your cancer type.

survivor

The fight against cancer is painful and continues long after treatment is complete.

Survivors will find joy as they inspire others by sharing their personal experiences and triumphs.

They will find solace as their questions and concerns are resolved during the transition into life after cancer, which carriers its own challenges.

supporter

Cancer is a battle fought by more than the individual alone.

Friends and family members struggle alongside their loved ones and can feel the pain just as deeply.

Cancer Spot allows supporters to identify themselves as parents, spouses/partners, siblings, children, doctors, nurses, or friends in order to share and uplift one another. Supporters of “Angels” will be able to connect with others to promote remembrance and healing.

Connect

The Cancer Spot algorithm generates a curated list of similar users based on shared characteristics, such as type of cancer, age and location.


Users will be connected to others who understand their unique circumstances and who are most likely to become meaningful relationships.

Message

After the connection is made, users will be given the opportunity to communicate and support each other through mobile messaging.


This way, users are only a few taps away from a kind piece of advice or listening ear of someone who knows their situation best.

Our Story

At age 12, Rowan was diagnosed with a rare type of bone cancer called Ewing’s-sarcoma. After a year of multiple surgeries and 15 rounds of chemotherapy, Rowan’s cancer went into hiding, only to return a year later — this time in his lungs. The bleak odds of surviving this devastating reoccurrence demanded drastic measures, and doctors responded by treating him with even more powerful chemotherapy, more surgeries, and eventually a stem cell transplant. This protocol, combined with a Heavenly intervention powered by the faith and prayers of his family and friends around the world, was a success. Rowan has been free of disease ever since.

Cancer Spot is Rowan’s way of giving back to God, his friends and the medical professionals who supported him, as well as to the many friends he lost on the way to winning his own fight against cancer. This is why Rowan teamed up with two of his lifelong friends, Spencer and Alex, to connect, unite and strengthen all those affected by cancer. The Cancer Spot team is dedicated to providing the life-sustaining tools of hope, optimism, and information, which we all need to defeat this merciless and indiscriminate killer.
